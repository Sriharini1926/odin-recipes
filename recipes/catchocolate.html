Cat chocolates

Description

Sweet and velvety chocolates paired with cute cat decorations.Who among us will say no?

Ingredients

  • Dark chocolates
  • buttercream icing
  • piping bag

Procedure

  1. Melt the dark chocolates in a double boiler and cat them in in silicon mould of desired shape
  2. Take the prepared chocolates out after refrigiration and ready the piping bags with buttercream icing of your choice
  3. Pipe the icing on the chocolates in the shape of a cat's face with two dots for eyes and whiskers and a smiling mouth
